Is Better Investor free to use?

Yes! Our retirement planner and investment calculators are completely free to use. No credit card required.

Do you provide personalized financial advice?

No. Better Investor provides educational tools and general information only. We are not financial advisors and do not provide personalized investment advice. Please consult with a qualified financial professional for advice tailored to your situation.

Do you store my financial data?

No. All calculations are performed locally in your browser. We do not store, transmit, or have access to the financial data you input into our calculators. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

How accurate are the calculators?

Our calculators use standard financial formulas and provide reasonable estimates. However, they are simplified models and cannot account for all real-world variables. Results should be used for educational purposes and rough planning only.

Can I use Better Investor if I'm not in the US?

Yes, but please note that our content is primarily designed for US residents. Tax implications, regulations, and recommendations may not apply to your jurisdiction.

Do you offer premium features or paid plans?

Currently, all our tools are free. If we introduce premium features in the future, we will announce them on our website.

How can I report a bug or suggest a feature?

We'd love to hear from you! Please email us at support@better-investor.co with details about the bug or your feature suggestion.
